# **App Name**: LCCI Live

## Core Features:

- Live Radio Player: An interactive audio player to stream LCCI's live broadcasts with play, pause, and volume controls.
- Mission & Programs Display: Dedicated sections presenting LCCI's mission, values, and detailed information about their programs and initiatives.
- Contact & Inquiry Form: A user-friendly form for visitors to submit inquiries, feedback, or messages directly to LCCI.
- Broadcast Schedule Viewer: A dynamic section showcasing the upcoming live broadcast schedule and program timings for LCCI.
- AI Chat Assistant: An AI-powered tool to provide instant answers to common questions about LCCI's mission, programs, and operations based on website content.

## Style Guidelines:

- A bold, modern blue (#00BFFF) to signify dynamism and clarity, making key interactive elements pop against a dark background.
- A deep, sophisticated charcoal black (#171C1C) with a subtle cyan undertone, providing a clean and professional dark canvas.
- A soft, off-white (#EFF3F3) used for main text, headings, and outlines, ensuring high readability and a sense of refinement against the dark backdrop.
- 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, objective, and highly legible aesthetic, suitable for both short headlines and detailed program descriptions.
- Utilize sleek, geometric line-based icons that complement the modern aesthetic, emphasizing clarity and minimalist design for actions and categories.
- Adopt a clean, spacious, and responsive layout, ensuring optimal readability and interaction across all devices. Prominently feature the radio player.
- Implement smooth, subtle transitions and hover effects for interactive elements, such as the radio player controls and navigation links, to enhance the user experience.
